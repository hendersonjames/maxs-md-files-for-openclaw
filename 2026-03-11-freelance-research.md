## Upwork + Fiverr: What Actually Makes Money Right Now

---

## The Honest Picture

Fiverr and Upwork can both work, but they serve different types of work and have different timelines to income. Here's the breakdown:

---

## UPWORK — Better for Bigger Checks, Slower to Start

**What's actually in demand (Upwork 2026 In-Demand Skills Report):**
- AI automation consulting — $50–$150/hr
- Full stack development — $75–$150/hr
- AI workflow setup (n8n, Zapier, Make) — $50–$100/hr
- Data analytics — $50–$100/hr
- Fastest growing: AI video generation/editing — +329% YoY

**Reality check:**
- Takes 2–4 months to build reputation with reviews
- First few jobs are usually lower rate to get the account going
- Once established: $2,000–$8,000/month is achievable for AI automation services
- Better for project-based work ($500–$5,000 contracts)

**James's angle:** AI automation setup for small businesses (lead gen systems, chatbots, workflow automation) — directly relevant to what we're building

---

## FIVERR — Faster to Start, Smaller per Gig, More Volume

**Top selling gigs in AI right now:**
1. **AI chatbot setup** — $150–$800/gig (hot category)
2. **AI automation workflows** (Zapier/Make/n8n) — $100–$500/gig
3. **AI prompt writing/optimization** — $50–$200/gig
4. **Lead generation research** — $50–$300/gig
5. **AI content strategy** — $100–$400/gig

**Reality check:**
- Fiverr is shifting upmarket — basic gigs ($5–$20) are dying
- Higher-value gigs ($200–$800) now dominate the platform
- Takes 4–8 weeks to get traction with new gigs
- More volume, smaller checks vs. Upwork

**James's angle:** "AI lead generation system setup for contractors" — $300–$800/gig. This directly leverages the lead gen app we're building.
