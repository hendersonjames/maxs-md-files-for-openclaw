## 💊 Pill Reminder App — Market Research

**Market size:** $1.2B in 2024 → projected $3.4B by 2033 (12.3% CAGR). Growing fast.

**Current players:** MediSafe, MyTherapy, DoseCast, CareClinic, MyDoses

**Real gaps in the market (opportunities for James's app):**
1. **Security is broken** — 84% of FDA-approved medication apps have security vulnerabilities. A genuinely secure app is a real differentiator.
2. **Caregiver connectivity** — Most apps do individual reminders. Family/caregiver oversight (adult children managing elderly parents' meds) is underserved.
3. **Senior-friendly UI** — Most apps are designed for tech-savvy users. Large buttons, simple navigation for older adults = huge underserved market.
4. **Pill counter + reminder combo** — Most apps only remind. An app that also tracks how many pills are left (so you know when to refill) is genuinely different.

**The angle:** James built it for himself — which means it probably solves *his* problem well. The question Wednesday is: what does it do that the existing apps don't?
