# PLATFORM-SPECIFIC NOTES

## Fiverr
- Post all 3 gigs immediately
- Start at listed prices — DO NOT discount on Fiverr (it signals low quality)
- Add a strong gig video if possible (even a 60-second Loom walkthrough)
- First 10 reviews are everything — consider doing 2-3 at a slight discount privately to get momentum
- Response time matters — keep it under 1 hour during business hours

## Upwork
- Build a complete profile first (photo, bio, portfolio with LeadHub + ChronaCare as examples)
- Start by bidding on jobs, not just waiting for the catalog to be found
- Target jobs under $500 first to build JSS (Job Success Score)
- Connects cost money — be selective, write custom proposals, don't blast
- Hourly rate: set at $65-85/hr

## Freelancer.com
- Set up profile with same bio and portfolio
- Bid on projects daily — volume matters more here than on Upwork
- Compete on clarity and specificity, not price — don't race to the bottom
- Preferred badge (paid) helps visibility — consider after first 5 reviews

Phone gig → upsell website → upsell automation → monthly retainer
Automation gig → upsell phone + website → monthly retainer

The goal: every client ends up on a monthly plan ($149-299/month) for ongoing AI services.
